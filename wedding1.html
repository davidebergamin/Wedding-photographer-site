MELISA & DOMINICK

A rustic countryside celebration in the heart of Tuscany

Their Story

Melisa and Dominick's wedding was a beautiful celebration of love set against the backdrop of the Tuscan countryside. The couple chose a charming rustic villa surrounded by olive groves and vineyards for their special day.

The ceremony took place in the villa's garden, with a stunning view of the rolling hills. Melisa walked down the aisle in a delicate lace gown, while Dominick waited with tears in his eyes. Their vows, written by themselves, moved everyone to tears.

The reception was held in the villa's courtyard, decorated with string lights, olive branches, and local wildflowers. Guests enjoyed a traditional Tuscan feast, featuring local wines and farm-to-table cuisine. As the sun set, the couple shared their first dance under the stars, surrounded by their loved ones.
