SOPHIA & MICHAEL

A celebration of elegance and romance at the historic Castello di Barolo in Piedmont. Wine tasting, Michelin-starred cuisine, and dancing under the stars.

The ceremony took place in the estate's private chapel, a 17th-century architectural gem with original frescoes and stained glass windows. The couple's vows echoed through the ancient stone walls, creating a moment of timeless beauty.

Following the ceremony, guests were treated to a wine tasting featuring the estate's award-winning vintages, followed by a formal dinner in the grand hall prepared by a Michelin-starred chef.

As night fell, the couple and their guests moved to the terrace for dancing under the stars, with string lights illuminating the ancient stone walls and the rolling hills of Piedmont providing a magical backdrop.
