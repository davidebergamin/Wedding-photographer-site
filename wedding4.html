OLIVIA & NOAH

An elegant destination wedding at Villa del Balbianello on Lake Como. New York couple, historic villa, terraced gardens, and a magical atmosphere.

Olivia and Noah traveled from New York to celebrate their love in one of Italy's most picturesque settings, surrounded by close friends and family. The historic villa with its terraced gardens overlooking the lake created a magical atmosphere for this sophisticated yet intimate celebration.

The ceremony took place on a terrace with panoramic views of Lake Como, with the couple exchanging vows as the sun began to set over the mountains. The golden light created a dreamlike atmosphere that perfectly captured the romance of the moment.

The reception featured a blend of Italian and American traditions, with a multi-course dinner featuring local specialties, followed by dancing that continued late into the night under a canopy of stars and twinkling lights.
