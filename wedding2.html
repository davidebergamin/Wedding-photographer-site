EMMA & JAMES

An intimate countryside wedding in the rolling hills of Tuscany at Villa Olivia. Traditional Italian feast, string lights, and dancing under the stars.

The ceremony took place in the villa's garden, with a stunning view of the Tuscan landscape. The couple exchanged vows under an arch of wildflowers, with their closest friends and family witnessing their special moment.

As the sun began to set, guests enjoyed a traditional Italian feast in the villa's courtyard, which was decorated with string lights and candles. The evening was filled with laughter, dancing, and heartfelt toasts to the newlyweds.

The celebration continued late into the night, with dancing under the stars and a surprise fireworks display that lit up the Tuscan sky.
